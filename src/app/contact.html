Contact the Modern Slavery Helpline Online

If you have concerns about someone you know, including a person in your family, you can use this form to tell the Modern Slavery Helpline.

One of our advisors will read it within 24 hours and decide what action needs to be taken. Alternatively, you can call us on 0800 0121 700*.

Modern Slavery Helpline Form

What we need from you

Please say as much as you can about the situation – it will help us take the right action. Tell us the facts that you know, as well as any fears you have about that person.

Giving us your details

It is very helpful to have your name and contact details. It may also help to protect someone if you give permission for the police or a social worker to contact you if they have any questions. However, you can remain anonymous if you prefer.

Immediate danger

If you think a person is in immediate danger, please call the emergency services on 999
